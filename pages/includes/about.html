Is my event big enough to have an entertainer?

Show can be customized to accommodate any group size or venue. Whether your event is for 30 people or a 1000 people, we can do it!

The show can be performed in a restaurant for small groups, banquet halls for larger audiences and theatres for a bigger production.

How long will the show be?

This Comedy/Magic Show can be customized to fit any time slot. The average time is 45 minutes to an 1 hour. The performance is successfully designed to only feel like 20 minutes and will always leave your guest wanting more.
